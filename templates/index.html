Expense Calculator is the easiest way to share bills with friends
Here's how it works:

Create your event

Simply create an Expense Calculator and add your expenses one at a time. You can also send invites to your friends before the trip so everyone can work on it together.

Share it

Once you're finished. send an email with a link to the event. Your friends can then enter what they paid.

Settle up

Everyone can see how much they owe and to whom. You can settle up at the end of the trip. or over time if needed.
